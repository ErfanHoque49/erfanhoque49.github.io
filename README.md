## 👨‍🔬 About Me

I am an **Assistant Professor of Bio/Statistics and data science** at the **University of Saskatchewan**, specializing in statistical methodology for complex and correlated data. My research focuses on longitudinal modeling, copula-based dependence structures, time series analysis, statistical/machine learning and applications in health and environmental sciences. My work integrates theoretical development with interdisciplinary applications to address real-world data challenges.
