Prospective Students

I have position openings for summer undergraduate students, MSc students, Ph.D. students, and Post-doc students. Please get in touch with me by email for detailed information. Ideal candidates should have strong theoretical foundations (BSc or MSc) in statistics/data science/biostatistics, excellent communication skills and proficiency in programming with R or Python. When reaching out regarding gradute opportunities, please include the following documents to support a productive discussion:

  • A brief curriculum vitae (CV)
  • An unofficial copy of your academic transcript
  • A research statement explaining your interest in our lab
  • For international students, a valid English language proficiency score. English proficiency requirement at USask
Please note that I will reply to your email if your credential matches with our need. Students with self-funding or Government-funding are also welcome. Thank you for your interest in our group.

Undergraduate Summer Research Opportunities: Senior undergraduate students (3rd year or above) with a background in statistics, biostatistics, data science, mathematics or computer science are invited to explore summer research opportunities (e.g. NSERC Undergraduate Student Research Award (USRA)). Projects will focus on topics of mutual interest, and students may also choose from a list of available research areas. Experience with R and Python is considered an asset. To facilitate productive discussions, interested students are encouraged to meet in person and bring:

  • A brief curriculum vitae (CV)
  • An unofficial copy of academic transcripts
